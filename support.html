FAQ
How do I deposit funds? ▼
Navigate to Deposit. Select your cryptocurrency, copy the address, and send funds. Deposits confirm within 10-30 minutes.
What are withdrawal fees? ▼
Fees vary by asset. BTC: 0.0005, ETH: 0.005, USDT: $5, SOL: 0.01. Minimum amounts apply.
How to enable 2FA? ▼
Go to Settings → Security → Enable 2FA. Scan QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy.
Minimum deposit? ▼
BTC: 0.001, ETH: 0.01, USDT: $50, SOL: 0.1. Below these may not be credited.
Withdrawal time? ▼
Processed within 24 hours after security review, plus network confirmations (10-60 min).
