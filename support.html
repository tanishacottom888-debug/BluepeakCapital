Frequently Asked Questions
How do I deposit funds into my account? ▼
Navigate to Wallet → Deposit. Select your preferred cryptocurrency, copy the deposit address, and send funds from your external wallet. Deposits typically confirm within 10-30 minutes.
What are the withdrawal fees? ▼
Withdrawal fees vary by asset. BTC: 0.0005, ETH: 0.005, USDT: $5, SOL: 0.01. Minimum withdrawal amounts apply. All fees are clearly shown before confirmation.
How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication? ▼
Go to Settings → Security → Enable 2FA. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy, then enter the verification code to complete setup.
What is the minimum deposit amount? ▼
Minimum deposits: BTC: 0.001, ETH: 0.01, USDT: $50, SOL: 0.1. Deposits below these amounts may not be credited to your account.
How long do withdrawals take? ▼
Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours after security review. Crypto withdrawals then require network confirmations (typically 10-60 minutes depending on the asset).
